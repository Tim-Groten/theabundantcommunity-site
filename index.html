Our Mission

Abundant Community exists to ensure youth aging out of foster care have the resources, support, and opportunities they need to thrive. We believe in building systems of abundance, not scarcity, through partnerships and community-driven initiatives.

Current Focus

We are actively building collaborations with housing organizations, educational programs, and job training networks to create a robust support web for youth exiting foster care.

Future Focus

In the coming year, we aim to expand our pilot programs nationwide, develop data-driven intervention models, and advocate for systemic policy reforms that address the long-term needs of aging-out youth.
