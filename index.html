Abundant Community

Removing barriers for people with complex needs by unlocking Houston’s hidden social and relational resources.

Our Mission

Abundant Community is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to helping individuals with complex needs gain direct access to the untapped social and relational resources of Houston. By connecting people to networks of care and opportunity, we aim to remove barriers that prevent them from thriving.

Current Focus

Our first target community is youth aging out of foster care. According to multiple studies, 31–46% of these young adults experience homelessness at least once by age 26. Abundant Community seeks to change that outcome by providing relationships, support, and connection.

Future Vision

In the future, we plan to expand our work to directly support those currently experiencing homelessness, helping them access both tangible resources and sustainable relationships that lead to long-term stability.
